About Us

SKYLL TECH was founded in 2023 by Godfrey Ramsey Chaula and Joseph Kabalula, two professionals with shining skills in GIS, statistical analysis, and environmental science. Having worked on diverse academic and project-based assignments, they created the company to offer high-quality consultancy and academic support, combining their expertise to guide clients through complex data-driven challenges.

As a consultancy, we deliver accurate, reproducible and well-documented analyses tailored to institutions, researchers and students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between data and decision-making, emphasising transparency and capacity-building; we provide not only reports but also the underlying code, data, and training needed for clients to sustain their projects.
