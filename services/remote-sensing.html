Remote Sensing & Geostatistics

Satellite/airborne imagery processing, classification, change detection. Geostatistical interpolation (kriging), spatial statistics and terrain analysis.

We handle multispectral and hyperspectral data, perform NDVI and other vegetation index calculations, and produce maps for agriculture, forestry and environmental monitoring.

  • Image preprocessing and enhancement
  • Land cover classification and change monitoring
  • Interpolation of environmental variables using kriging
  • Terrain modelling and slope/aspect analysis
  • Time-series analysis of remote sensing data

Our geostatistical models are tailored to your data’s spatial structure, helping you make reliable predictions and decisions.
